Amina Learn-2-Earn

Amina grades labellers as Level 1 to Level 7. Level 1 can earn Amina rewards for learning to label. After passing the on-boarding test, Level 0 labellers can be assigned L1 labeling tasks. Each label should develop their labeling proficiency through accumulating hours of labelling experience. The highest level is Level 7. The higher the level of the labeller, the higher the Amina rewards portions.

Amina Rewards Wallet

Amina rewards are Amina tokens awarded to labellers and participants of the Amina labeling platform. The rewards are awarded for a broad range of contributions including completing image labeling tasks, reviewing and checking accuracy of labels, rating checkers on their reviews, planners and other contributors such as data owners who provide labelled data to the community.
